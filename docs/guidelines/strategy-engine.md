# Pitch Simulator Strategy Engine

## 1. The Core Objective
The Bridge2Partners interactive pitch simulator isn't just an aesthetics tool—it's a psychological combat simulator for managing senior banking executives. Every visual element has a corresponding "Challenger Sale" cognitive anchor.

## 2. No-Read Policy (Cognitive Load Constraints)
The human brain cannot simultaneously process auditory complex data and read paragraphs of text.
**Constraint Rule:**
- **Slides 1 & 2:** Absolutely zero body copy. 8-10 words maximum. The words are traps designed to be comprehended in <2 seconds.
- **Slides 3+:** Body copy is permissible ONLY if it acts as a strategy anchor (e.g., "$80B", "40+ Integrations").

## 3. Persona Mapping & Objections
The AI simulates specific executive archetypes:
*   **The CIO:** Cares about tech debt, legacy bindings, and architecture (Hub-and-Spoke slides).
*   **The Head of Lending:** Cares about time-to-market and onboarding friction (The Insiders slide/domain expertise).
*   **The EVP of Retail:** Cares about breaking the "Status Quo Bias" without regulatory implosions.

## 4. The Transition Anchors
Every slide contains a visual anchor line that serves to link the logic between stages.
- The `bg-secondary` (Teal) anchor sits at exactly `bottom-[5.5cqw]` and represents the "Baseline Truth."
- Images strictly sit FLUSH with this line (`bottom-[5.65cqw]`) to symbolize precision engineering and operational confidence.
